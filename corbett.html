Corbett Mussoorie Nainital Tour

Overview

Corbett Mussoorie Nainital Tour ‚Äì Our Corbett Mussoorie and Nainital Tour Package lets you explore the various gems embedded in the treasure trove of the Kumaon Hills. Away from the hustle and bustle of the city, Corbett National Park, Nainital and Mussoorie are some of the beautiful places to visit in the lap of nature. With our tour packages you get the opportunity to enjoy the magnificence of all these amazing places.

Itinerary

  • Day 1 Delhi - Corbett (270 kms/6 hrs)
  • Day 2 Corbett
    • After breakfast proceed for jungle safari. Many of the wild creatures are more active during dawn. after visit return to the hotel for lunch and thereafter enjoy jeep safari in various nooks and corners of the park. Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 3 Corbett - Nainital (80 kms/3hrs)
    • After breakfast drive to Nainital. Upon arrival, check into the hotel. Later visit tourist charms of the place like the Hanuman Giri, Golf Course and Naina Devi Temple. Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 4 Nainital
    • After breakfast visit famous lakes of Nainital. We will take you to Naukuchia Tal, Malli Tal, Bhim Tal, Sattal and Khurpa Tal. Afternoon you can enjoy boat ride on the Naini Lake. Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 5 Nainital ‚Äì Mussoorie (295 kms/8hrs)
    • After breakfast drive to Mussoorie. On arrival transfer to a hotel, rest of the day free at leisure. Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 6 Mussoorie
    • After breakfast visit various famous tourist charms of Mussoorie. Later different destinations such as Kempty Fall, Mussoorie Fall, Van Chetna Kendra, Camel Back and Surkanda Devi Temple. Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 7 Mussoorie - Delhi, Departure (315 kms/8hrs)
    • After breakfast drive back to Delhi and on arrival transfer to airport or railway station for your own destination.
