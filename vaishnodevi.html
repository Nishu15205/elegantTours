Mata Vaishno Devi Darshan

Overview

Mata Vaishno Devi Darshan is one of the powerful holy pilgrimage and most famous religious place in North India. Embark on a spiritual journey to the main temple to seek blessings with our tour package with Maa Vaishno Devi. The holy abode of Maa Vaishno Devi Teerth is situated on top of the Trikuta hills in the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir.
We also provide Vaishno Devi Yatra by Helicopter, which makes traveling easy and saves time. Trekking is the most commonly used method by the devotees to reach the holy temple. One has to trek for about 14 kms to reach the temple. You will find many exciting stops along this long route. We take care of food, stay and transfer for a relaxing pilgrimage experience.
