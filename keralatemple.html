Temple Tour Kerala

Overview

Temple Tour Kerala is not only a treat for the eyes but also for the body, mind and soul. The people of Kerala believe that the 5,000-year-old medical system of Ayurveda can cure any disease and unlike other states in India, this southern state of India takes it as a mainstream medical treatment, not an alternative . The reason being the gateway of Ayurveda tourism is that it is blessed with an even climate, natural abundance of forests and cool monsoon weather, which is perfect for any treatment.
