Motorbike Safari Ladakh

Overview

Motorbike Safari Ladakh ‚Äì Get ready to make your dream of riding in Ladakh come true on an action-packed bike trip where you will experience the amalgamation of adventure, serene beauty and culture of the ‚ÄúLand of High Passes‚Äù. This tour offers you a stay amidst the beautiful valley of the mountains and the starry sky at night. The unique thing about Ladakh region is that it has mountains, forests, deserts, lakes, rivers, waterfalls in a beautiful and dynamic geography.

Itinerary

  • Day 01 Delhi ‚Äì Leh
  • Day 02
    • After breakfast, attend a short briefing and collect your bikes. Later drive to Thiksey Monastery, which is one of the most beautiful monasteries of Ladakh belonging to the Gelugpa Order of Buddhism. After this, we shall ride to Shey Palace, which is located 15 km to the south of Leh on the Leh-Manali highway. After lunch, visit Shanti Stupa and Leh Palace. Overnight at the hotel.
  • Day 03 Leh - Khardung La Pass - Nubra Valley
    • After breakfast, ride to Nubra Valley that lies in the north of Leh and is accessible via Khardung la (5,570 Mts.), which is the highest motorable roads in the world. Reach Sumur or Hunder by afternoon and rest of the day explore beautiful village of Sumur and Hunder with a Visit to Diskit Monastery. Overnight at Camp.
  • Day 04 Nubra Valley
    • After breakfast, visit the sand dunes at Hunder and enjoy a camel safari and later drive back to Leh for the overnight at the Camp.
  • Day 05 Leh - Likir - Alchi - Uletokpo
    • After breakfast, drive to Likir Gompa, which lies on the right side of Leh-Srinagar road about 4 Kms from the main road. Further we will bike to Alchi Gompa, where we can witness unique paintings attributed from the mid to late 11th century and constructed by the famous translator Rinchen Zangpo. Later drive to Uletokpo. Overnight at the camp.
  • Day 06 Uletokpo - Lamayuru - Kargil
    • After breakfast, drive to Kargil and on the way visit Lamayuru monastery which is the oldest monastery in Ladakh. The bike over the Namik-la (3,697 Mts.) to reach Mulbekh where there is a Chamba statue that has been carved out of a single rock of approximately 50 ft. height, over Fotu-la (4,087 Mts.), the highest pass on Leh ‚Äì Srinagar road. Driving down the kangaroo curves, we shall reach Kargil. Overnight at the hotel.
  • Day 07 Kargil - Sonamarg
    • After breakfast, ride to Srinagar via Drass (Second Coldest inhabited place in the World), en route we will visit Kargil war museum, Minimarg and cross Zojila (3033 Mts.) to reach a beautiful destination of Sonamarg in Kashmir Valley. After crossing the Jimmy Gate on Zojila we will enter Kashmir Valley and then we will head out to Srinagar in the evening and check into a house boat on famous Dal Lake. Overenight in houseboat.
  • Day 08 Srinagar
    • After breakfast an excursion to Shankaracharya Temple and Mughal Gardens, Nishat Bagh (Garden of Pleasure) and Shalimar Bagh (Garden of Pleasure). After visiting these places, we shall bike to Gulmarg, which is a world renowned ski resort. In the evening, we will get back to our houseboat and make a small excursion on Shikara (boats) to the floating market in Dal Lake. Overnight in houseboat
  • Day 09 Srinagar ‚Äì Delhi
    • After breakfast, leave for Srinagar airport from where we will board a flight to Delhi for your ownward journey.
  • Day 10
    • Upon arrival in Delhi, continue for your own destination
