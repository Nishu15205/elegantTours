Overview

Itinerary

  • Day 01: Arrive Kochi ‚Äì Munnar
  • Day 02: Munnar
  • After breakfast, full-day sightseeing around Munnar.
  • Enjoy panoramic views of Anamudi, the highest peak of South India at 2695 m, visible from Munnar.
  • Visit Christ Church and proceed for a wildlife expedition at Eravikulam National Park (16 km).
  • Continue the tour in Rajamalai Hills, adjacent to the park.
  • Stop by Madupetty Dam, a famous picnic spot for boating and horse riding.
  • Later in the evening, transfer to the hotel. Overnight at the hotel.
  • Day 03: Munnar - Thekkady - Periyar
  • After breakfast, drive to Thekkady.
  • On arrival at Thekkady, check in at the hotel.
  • Later, enjoy a game viewing cruise on Periyar Lake.
  • Spot a variety of wildlife including elephants, monkeys, deer, wild boar, and unique birds.
  • Return to the hotel. Overnight at the hotel.
  • Day 04: Periyar - Kumarakom
  • After breakfast, proceed to Kumarakom.
  • Check into the hotel/resort.
  • Visit Vembanad Lake, a serene location perfect for relaxation.
  • Rest of the day at leisure. Overnight at the hotel/resort.
  • Day 05: Kumarakom
  • After breakfast, enjoy a free day at leisure.
  • Overnight at the hotel.
  • Day 06: Kumarakom - Alleppey
  • After breakfast, drive to Alleppey.
  • Experience the magic of the backwaters on a houseboat (Kettuvallam) cruise.
  • Spend the next 22 hours in your floating villa.
  • Get a glimpse of village life up close, a unique and unforgettable experience.
  • Enjoy the sunset. Overnight on the houseboat.
  • Day 07: Alleppey - Kochi Departure
  • After breakfast, drive back to Kochi International Airport for your onward journey.
