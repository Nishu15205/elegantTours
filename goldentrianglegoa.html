Golden Triangle Tour with Goa

Overview

Golden Triangle Tour with Goa ‚Äì Welcome to the most popular tourist circuit of India. Journey across the fascinating world of erstwhile Mughals and Maharajas and get a peek into some of the best architecture marvels, including Qutab Minar, Amber Fort and the famed Taj Mahal. This tour will take you to the serene and clean beaches of Goa apart from Delhi Rajasthan so that you can experience ultimate bliss and take home many memories to last a lifetime.

Itinerary

  • Day 01: Arrive Delhi
  • Upon arrival, after clearing immigration and customs, you will be met and transferred to your hotel.
  • Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 02: Delhi
  • Following breakfast, enjoy a full-day city tour of Old & New Delhi.
  • Old Delhi: An ancient walled city. Drive past the Red Fort ‚Äì the opulent fort and palace of the Mughal Empire; Raj Ghat, the memorial site of Mahatma Gandhi; Jama Masjid, the largest mosque in India; and Chandni Chowk, the bustling and colorful market of the old city (Red Fort is closed on Mondays).
  • Afternoon, New Delhi: Visit Humayun‚Äôs Tomb (1586), built by Haji Begum (wife of Humayun) ‚Äì an early example of Moghul architecture, the Qutab Minar (72 meters high), and the Iron Pillar, which has withstood 1500 years of weather. Continue with a drive through the Embassy area and government buildings (1921-1930).
  • Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 03: Delhi - Agra
  • Following breakfast, drive to Agra and check in to your hotel.
  • In the afternoon, visit the Taj Mahal and Agra Fort.
  • Taj Mahal: Agra‚Äôs famous 17th-century masterpiece, built in memory of Mumtaz ‚Äì the most splendid architectural monument in the world. (Note: Taj Mahal is closed on Fridays.)
  • Agra City: Also visit the Mughal Red Fort (Agra Fort), built by Akbar (1565-1573 A.D.), featuring gateways, pillared halls, mosques, marble trellises, and geometric gardens that represent Mughal architecture at its peak.
  • Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 04: Agra - Fatehpur Sikri - Jaipur
  • Following breakfast, drive to Jaipur with an en-route visit to Fatehpur Sikri (the City of Victory) for a tour of this magnificent, well-preserved World Heritage site. Explore its religious and secular sections, where breathtaking sculpture and an incredible white marble mosque attract pilgrims from near and far.
  • Continue your drive to Jaipur and check in to your hotel.
  • Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 05: Jaipur
  • After breakfast, visit Amber (Amer) Fort ‚Äì ascend the hilltop fortress-palace on an elephant, the former capital of the royal Rajputs. Enjoy panoramic views from this architectural masterpiece, with its fortified battlements, sepia-hued hills overlooking Maota Lake, and intricate mirror-studded alcoves and dazzling mosaics.
  • In the afternoon, explore Jaipur City: Visit Hawa Mahal (Palace of the Winds), a five-storied pink sandstone structure with 593 delicately carved stone screens, and join a guided tour of the City Palace & Observatory, which blend Rajasthani and Mughal architecture.
  • Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 06: Jaipur - Goa
  • After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your connecting flight to Goa.
  • Upon arrival in Goa, meet and transfer to your hotel.
  • Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 07: Goa
  • After breakfast, enjoy a full day free for your own activities.
  • Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 08: Goa
  • After breakfast, enjoy another full day free for your own activities.
  • Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 09: Goa
  • After breakfast, enjoy a full day free for your own activities.
  • Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 10: Goa - Delhi
  • After breakfast, enjoy a free morning for your own activities.
  • Later, transfer to the airport for your connecting flight to Delhi.
  • Upon arrival in Delhi, meet and transfer to your hotel.
  • Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 11: Delhi Departure
  • After breakfast, transfer to the international airport for your onward journey.
