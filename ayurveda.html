Ayurveda Tour Kerala

Overview

Ayurveda Tour Kerala is not only a treat for the eyes but also for the body, mind and soul. The people of Kerala believe that the 5,000-year-old medical system of Ayurveda can cure any disease and unlike other states in India, this southern state of India takes it as a mainstream medical treatment, not an alternative . The reason being the gateway of Ayurveda Tour Kerala is that it is blessed with an even climate, natural abundance of forests and cool monsoon weather, which is perfect for any treatment.

    • Day 01: Arrival Trivandrum
    • Day 02: Trivandrum - Alleppey
      • After breakfast, explore the spectacular beauty of Kerala by houseboats.
      • The houseboats of Kerala, known as Kettuvallam, are made without using nails.
      • These houseboats have been adapted into luxuriously furnished accommodations.
      • Drive to Thottapally and board a houseboat.
      • Enjoy lunch and dinner on board while traveling through the backwaters to Alleppey.
      • Overnight in the houseboat.
    • Day 03: Alleppey - Kumarakom
      • After breakfast, disembark the houseboat and drive to Kumarakom.
      • Kumarakom is a tourist paradise and a habitat for several migratory birds.
      • The area provides a safe nesting ground with access to Vembanad Lake.
      • Overnight at the hotel.
    • Day 04: Kumarakom - Periyar
      • After breakfast, drive to Periyar, known for its wildlife and scenic beauty.
      • In the evening, enjoy a boat ride at the Periyar Lake while looking for animals on the shore.
      • Overnight at the hotel.
    • Day 05: Periyar - Munnar
      • After breakfast, explore the wildlife and natural beauty of Periyar.
      • Later, drive to Munnar, a peaceful and beautiful hill station.
      • Visit the National Park and take a tour of the tea plantations.
      • Overnight at the hotel.
    • Day 06: Munnar - Cochin
      • After breakfast, drive to Cochin.
      • Afternoon city tour includes Fort Cochin, Santa Cruz Basilica, St. Francis Church, and the famous Chinese fishing nets.
      • Visit the Dutch Palace at Mattancherry and the Paradeshi Synagogue.
      • Evening, enjoy a Kathakali Dance performance.
      • Overnight at the hotel.
    • Day 07: Cochin - Kovalam
      • After breakfast, drive to Kovalam Beach and check into the Ayurvedic Treatment Center.
      • Kovalam is a renowned beach destination with several Ayurvedic resorts.
      • Overnight at Kovalam.
    • Day 08 to 14: Kovalam Ayurvedic Treatment Center
      • Undergo a seven-day Ayurvedic treatment program as prescribed by the doctor.
    • Day 09: Kovalam - Trivandrum Departure
      • After breakfast, transfer to Trivandrum Airport for departure.
