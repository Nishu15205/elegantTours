Beas Kund Trek Himachal

Overview

Beas Kund Trek Himachal has always been a famous trek around Manali. Eventually, it takes you to the source of the river that flows through the city. But one reason why trekkers love this trek is the setting. Lush meadows rise under the peaks of the three largest mountains around Manali. And hidden in these grasslands is the emerald lake from which the river Beas originates.
Nestled amidst the jagged mountain peaks of the Pir Panjal range, the serene view of this serene water body adds to its beauty.

Itinerary

  • Day 01: Arrival in Manali
  • Upon arrival in Manali, meet and transfer to Camp.
  • Rest of the day at leisure.
  • Evening free to explore Mall Road, where you can visit some of the best cafes in Manali or indulge in a shopping spree.
  • Later, return back to the camp.
  • Overnight in Camp.
  • Day 02: Solang Valley - Jaralu Thach - Dhundi (8 kms in 4 hours)
  • After breakfast, move to a pre-determined spot in Solang Valley.
  • The trek leader will give a brief introduction to the trek and start trekking towards Bakar/Jaralu Thach, located at an altitude of 3149 m.
  • The trek is around 8 km long with a gradual incline, passing through towering fir trees and dense oak and walnut forests.
  • Enjoy the fresh forest fragrance and the silence, except for occasional birds and your footsteps.
  • Soak in the magnificent views of the Himalayas, specifically the enchanting sight of Hanuman Tibba in the distance.
  • On reaching Dhundi, set up camp.
  • Overnight in the Camp.
  • Day 03: Dhundi - Beas Kund - Dhundi (12 kms, 8 - 10 hours)
  • After breakfast, begin the trek to Beas Kund, a slightly strenuous yet rewarding hike.
  • Marvel at the lush vistas of the snow-capped Himalayas like Deo Tibba and Indrasen.
  • As you ascend, the vegetation becomes sparse, and you can see the glacier from a distance.
  • After trekking for 4-5 hours, reach Beas Kund, a pristine lake with striking blue waters surrounded by high mountains and occasional wildflowers.
  • The lake is the source of the Beas River, and you can observe how small streams merge into the main river.
  • Spend time soaking in the breathtaking views and clicking pictures.
  • After a while, begin the trek back to Dhundi, which takes another 4-5 hours.
  • Upon reaching the campsite, relax and unwind.
  • Overnight in the tent.
  • Day 04: Dhundi - Solang Valley - Manali
  • After breakfast, start the descent back to Solang Valley, which will take around 4 hours at a comfortable pace.
  • Upon reaching Solang Valley, return to Manali by bus or taxi for your onward journey.
